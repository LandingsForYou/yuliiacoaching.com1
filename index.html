    Frequently Asked Questions

    I am a Life & Business Coach working in accordance with ICF standards. My professional background includes more than 10 years of experience in audit and business, as well as individual and group coaching practice.

    I work with entrepreneurs, executives, professionals, and individuals who are at a point of growth or change and want to make more conscious and well-considered decisions in their professional and personal lives.

    Coaching is suitable for those who:

    • • are ready to work with their own goals and decisions;
    • • seek clarity, structure, and forward movement;
    • • take responsibility for changes in their life or work .

    Coaching does not provide ready-made advice or instructions and is not therapy.

    Coaching takes place in individual 1:1 sessions. The work is built around your request and adapted to your current situation and goals.

    Sessions are structured dialogues aimed at clarifying situations, supporting decision-making, and defining next steps.

    Coaching is focused on the present and the future rather than on analysing the past. A coach does not give advice, evaluate, or offer ready-made solutions.

    Coaching is a partnership-based process that helps you better understand your situation, find your own answers, and act consciously.

    Sessions are held online or in person — by agreement.
    The duration of one session is 60 minutes.

    The work begins with an introductory consultation, where we discuss your request and the format of cooperation.

    Further steps are agreed individually after the first meeting.
    Payment is made by invoice.

    For some topics, a few sessions are sufficient; in other cases, the work may be longer-term.

    The format and duration of the cooperation are defined jointly, based on the goals and the dynamics of the process.

    Individual Coaching (1:1)

    Personal coaching sessions fully focused on your request, goals, and decisions.

    This format is suitable for professional and personal topics that require attention, structure, and an individual approach.

    Group Coaching

    Coaching in a small group with a shared focus or theme.
